The most important decisions:

1. Use an explicit durable graph instead of an open-ended agent swarm.
2. Make the story bible and artifact lineage the center of memory.
3. Treat every agent call as a budgeted, observable, reproducible operation.
4. Make local/cloud/hybrid configurations first-class product features.
5. Evaluate creative quality with blind human comparisons.
6. Keep the no-editor philosophy, but provide excellent artifact review, diffing, branching, and chat-driven revision.
